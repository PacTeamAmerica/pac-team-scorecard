About this scorecard  ·  what it shows and how it works ▼

The Commercial Scorecard gives PAC TEAM a single, living view of where revenue stands across every strategic account — what has been earned, what is committed, and what is still in play.

Each account carries one score, built from three layers of decreasing certainty:

  • Invoiced YTD — revenue already recognized this year, net of credit memos, from NetSuite.
  • Open orders — won but not yet billed: the unbilled value of open sales orders.
  • Weighted pipeline — future opportunities from Podio, discounted by how likely each is to close: 80%+ counts in full, 50–79% at half, below 50% not at all.

Accounts sit in three tiers — Key accounts (the largest, defended relationships), Strategic growth (accounts with room to scale), and Strategic targets (CEO-approved net-new names). The active-client count tracks concentration: the strategy favors fewer, deeper relationships, so a smaller number is the goal.

Each Monday, three exports — NetSuite invoiced revenue, NetSuite open orders, and the Podio pipeline — are dropped into the panel below and every figure recomputes instantly. Between uploads, the scorecard runs on the last numbers built in, so it always shows something real.
